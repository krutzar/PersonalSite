My Role



As the Marketing Coordinator at Fibonacci, I focus on sharing the brewery's community messaging through social media content, email newsletters, and event planning.

About Fibonacci



Fibonacci Brewing Company is a nanobrewery that produces high quality craft beers in a laid back, casual environment in Mt Healthy, Ohio. Our entire craft beer lineup is available for consumption on site, while growlers to go is subject to availability. We produce a wide variety of craft beers, concentrating on non-traditional and hybrid beer styles, that appeal to all beer drinkers ranging from casual beer drinkers to the beer connoisseur.

Each week Bearcat Launchpad brought in a local entrepreneur to discuss a different topic. While I wasn't working on a specific company at the time, I found myself consuming as much information as possible.

Creative Problem Solving

Creative problem solving is an essential skill for any entrepreneur or high performing employee. Running Bearcat Launchpad not only provided me with lots of external examples and lessons to learn from, but it also provided plenty of firsthand problems within the organization to test this knowledge on.

My Role



As the Vice President/Marketing and Operstions Manager I was responsible for much of the scheduling and coordination surrounding workshops, speakers, and events. A large part of our mission however was to refine the organization to resemble more of an accelerator program instead of a speaker series. This meant there was lots of work on the back end to reform the structure and funding procedure for the organization, which was much easier said than done.

About Bearcat Launchpad



Bearcat Launchpad is UC's Student led business accelerator! The organization helps fuel student ventures by providing mentorship, funding and education to help connect students to the #StartupCincy ecosystem.
